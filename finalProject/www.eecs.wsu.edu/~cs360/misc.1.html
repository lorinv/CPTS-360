360 PROJECT HELP

                     Misc Level-1 Functions

These inlcude 

touch(pathname) : modify the INODE's i_atime and i_mtime fields.


chmod(newMode, pathname): modify INODE.i_mode's permission bits
                          You may specify newMode in Octal form, e.g. 0644
                          or you may use +x, -r, +w, etc fancier formats.

chown(newOwner, pathname): change INODE.i_uid to newOwner
 

chgrp(newGroup, pathname): change INODE.i_gid to newGroup
